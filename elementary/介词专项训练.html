介词专项训练学案

Prepositions

一、概念说明

介词用于表示名词或代词与其他词之间的关系，常见分类如下：

1. 方位介词（表示位置）

介词意思例句
in在……里面The book is in the bag.
on在……上面The cat is on the table.
under在……下面The ball is under the chair.
behind在……后面The boy is behind the door.
in front of在……前面The car is in front of the house.
next to在……旁边The school is next to the park.
between在……之间（两者）The girl is between her parents.
near在……附近The supermarket is near my home.

2. 时间介词（表示时间）

介词用法例句
at具体时间点at 7 o'clock / at noon
on具体某天/日期on Monday / on May 1st
in月份/年份/季节/一天中的时间段in March / in 2024 / in summer / in the morning

3. 其他常见介词

介词意思例句
with和……一起I go to school with my friend.
for为了/给This gift is for you.
from从……I am from China.
to到/去……She goes to the library.

二、翻译练习——将下列句子翻译成英文

  1. 猫在桌子下面。

→ The cat is under the table.

  1. 你的书在书包里。

→ Your book is in the bag.

  1. 学校在公园旁边。

→ The school is next to the park.

  1. 我早上七点起床。

→ I get up at 7 o'clock in the morning.

  1. 她星期天去游泳。

→ She goes swimming on Sunday.

  1. 我妈妈是位老师。

→ My mother is a teacher.

三、选词填空——用 in / on / under / behind / next to / between 填空

  1. The cat is sleeping ___________ the bed.
  2. There is a book ___________ the desk.
  3. The ball is ___________ the chair.
  4. The boy is hiding ___________ the door.
  5. My house is ___________ the library and the supermarket.
  6. The park is ___________ my school.

四、选词填空——用 at / on / in 填空

  1. I get up ___________ 7:00 every day.
  2. We have English class ___________ Monday.
  3. My birthday is ___________ May.
  4. She goes to bed ___________ night.
  5. We play football ___________ the afternoon.
  6. They go to the park ___________ Sunday morning.

五、选择填空

  1. The cat is ___________ the table. （A. in B. on C. under）
  2. My school is ___________ the park. （A. next to B. in C. on）
  3. I go to school ___________ 8:00. （A. on B. at C. in）
  4. We have PE class ___________ Friday. （A. at B. on C. in）
  5. The girl is ___________ her parents. （A. between B. on C. under）
  6. I go to the library ___________ my friend. （A. for B. with C. from）

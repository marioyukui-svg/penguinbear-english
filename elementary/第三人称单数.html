To make the third person singular form of a verb in the present simple, following these spelling rules.

rulethird person singular verb form
直接加s如果一个动词以辅音或者辅音的声音结尾cook-cooks；live-lives
add-es如果动词以-s、-z ，-x，-ch或者-sh结尾pass-passes；relax-relaxes；watch-watches；wash-washes
把y替换成ies当动词以辅音+y结尾study-studies
加-es如果动词以元音声音结尾（a、e、i、o、u）say-says
加-s如果动词以元音+y结尾say-says

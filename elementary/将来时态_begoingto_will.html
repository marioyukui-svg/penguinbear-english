将来时态学案：be going to vs. will

Future Tense: "be going to" vs. "will"

一、概念说明

1. will

  • **will + 动词原形**，表示将来发生的动作或状态。
  • 常用于：

- 表示预测、推测

- 表示临时决定

- 表示意愿、承诺

否定形式： won't = will not

例句：

  • At 11 o'clock tomorrow, she **will be** at work.
  • Don't drink coffee before you go to bed. **You won't sleep.**
  • I am home now. → I **will be** home soon.（即将在家）
  • I go home now. → I **will go** home tomorrow.（即将回家）

注意： will + 动词原形，will 后面的动词永远用原形。

2. be going to

  • **be动词 + going to + 动词原形**，表示：

- 已经计划好、打算做的事情

- 根据迹象判断马上要发生的事情

常见句子结构：

人称肯定否定疑问
II am going to + 动词原形I am not going to + 动词原形Am I going to + 动词原形?
YouYou are going to + 动词原形You aren't going to + 动词原形Are you going to + 动词原形?
He/She/ItHe is going to + 动词原形He isn't going to + 动词原形Is he going to + 动词原形?
We/TheyThey are going to + 动词原形They aren't going to + 动词原形Are they going to + 动词原形?

例句：

  • I'm **going to buy** some books tomorrow.
  • Look at the sky. It's **going to rain**.
  • What are you **going to wear** tonight? I'm **going to wear** a T-shirt.
  • I'm **going to go out** with my friend.

注意： be going to 后面也可以接 go，不冲突。例如：I'm going to go to school.

3. be going to 与 will 的区别

比较项be going towill
计划性表示事先计划、打算做的事情表示临时决定或意愿
客观依据根据现有迹象判断马上发生表示主观推测
时间远近常表示较近的将来（马上）可表示较远或将来的任何时间

对比例句：

  • I'm **going to** visit my grandma this weekend.（早就计划好的）
  • OK, I **will** help you with your homework.（临时答应的）
  • Look at those clouds! It's **going to** rain.（看到云了）
  • I think it **will** rain tomorrow.（只是猜测）

二、翻译练习——将下列句子翻译成英文

  1. 明天十一点，她将在上班。

→ At 11 o'clock tomorrow, she will be at work.

  1. 我即将回家。

→ I will go home. / I am going to go home.

  1. 我明天要去买一些书。

→ I'm going to buy some books tomorrow.

  1. 看天空，快要下雨了。

→ Look at the sky. It's going to rain.

  1. 你今晚打算穿什么？

→ What are you going to wear tonight?

  1. 我打算和我的朋友出去。

→ I'm going to go out with my friend.

三、选词填空——用 will 或 be going to 的适当形式填空

  1. Look at the clouds! It ___________ (rain).
  2. I ___________ (visit) my grandma next Sunday.（计划好了）
  3. A: I'm thirsty. B: I ___________ (get) you some water.（临时决定）
  4. She ___________ (buy) a new dress tomorrow.（计划好了）
  5. I think he ___________ (be) a doctor when he grows up.（推测）
  6. They ___________ (not / have) a party this weekend.（计划取消）

四、选择填空

  1. I __________ a new bike next week. （A. will buy B. am going to buy C. all are correct）
  2. Look at those clouds! It __________ soon. （A. will rain B. is going to rain C. rains）
  3. A: I forgot my pencil. B: Don't worry, I __________ you one. （A. will lend B. am going to lend C. lend）
  4. She __________ her grandmother this weekend. （A. will visit B. is going to visit C. all are correct）

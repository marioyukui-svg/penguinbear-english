形容词性物主代词（my, your, his, her, its, our, their） 名词性物主代词（mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs）

物主代词学案

Possessive Pronouns: my / your / his / her / its / our / their & mine / yours / his / hers / its / ours / theirs

一、概念说明

  • 形容词性物主代词：表示“谁的”，后面必须接名词。

例如：my book（我的书）、your pen（你的笔）

  • 名词性物主代词：表示“……的东西”，后面不能接名词，它自己就是完整的意思。

例如：mine = my book / my pen（我的东西）

人称对应表：

人称形容词性物主代词名词性物主代词
Imy（我的）mine
Youyour（你的）yours
Hehis（他的）his
Sheher（她的）hers
Itits（它的）its
Weour（我们的）ours
Theytheir（他们的）theirs

用法对比：

  • This is my book. → This book is mine.
  • That is your pencil. → That pencil is yours.
  • These are their bags. → These bags are theirs.

三、选词填空——用 my / your / his / her / its / our / their 填空

  2. ___________ cat is very cute. （她的）
  3. We love ___________ school. （我们的）
  4. ___________ name is Tom. （他的）
  5. The dog is playing with ___________ bone. （它的）
  6. Please put away ___________ toys. （你们的）
  7. ___________ mother is a teacher. （他们的）

二、规则动词过去式（-ed结尾）

规则：

  • 大部分动词直接加 -ed：`play → played`
  • 以e结尾加 -d：`like → liked`
  • 辅音+y结尾，变y为i加 -ed：`study → studied`
  • 重读闭音节，双写最后一个字母加 -ed：`stop → stopped`

需要掌握的规则动词：

三、专项练习

练习1：写出过去式（填空）

把括号里的词变成过去式：

  1. Yesterday I ______ (see) a big dog.
  2. She ______ (go) to the park.
  3. He ______ (eat) a banana.
  4. We ______ (have) fun at school.
  5. I ______ (do) my homework.
  6. Mom ______ (make) dinner.
  7. The cat ______ (drink) milk.
  8. They ______ (swim) in the sea.
  9. She ______ (sing) a song.
  10. He ______ (run) to the bus.

答案：

  1. saw
  2. went
  3. ate
  4. had
  5. did
  6. made
  7. drank
  8. swam
  9. sang
  10. ran

练习2：选词填空（saw / went / ate / had / did）

从方框里选一个词填空：

sawwentatehaddid
  1. I ______ a movie yesterday.
  2. She ______ to the zoo.
  3. He ______ an apple for lunch.
  4. We ______ a good time.
  5. They ______ their homework.

答案：

  1. saw
  2. went
  3. ate
  4. had
  5. did

练习3：改错（找出错误并改正）

下列句子中过去式用错了，请改正：

  1. I seed a bird. → I ______ a bird.
  2. She goed to school. → She ______ to school.
  3. He eated rice. → He ______ rice.
  4. We haved a cat. → We ______ a cat.
  5. They doed it. → They ______ it.

答案：

  1. saw
  2. went
  3. ate
  4. had
  5. did

练习4：把句子改成过去式

把下列句子从一般现在时改成一般过去时（注意时间词也要改）：

示例：

  • 现在：I see a cat every day.
  • 过去：I saw a cat yesterday.
  1. She goes to school every day.

→ She ______ to school ______.

  1. He eats an apple every morning.

→ He ______ an apple ______.

  1. We have a party every weekend.

→ We ______ a party ______.

  1. They play football every afternoon.

→ They ______ football ______.

  1. I drink milk every night.

→ I ______ milk ______.

答案：

  1. went ... yesterday
  2. ate ... yesterday morning
  3. had ... last weekend
  4. played ... yesterday afternoon
  5. drank ... last night

四、每日小任务（5分钟）

任务：过去式小日记

每天写1-2个句子，描述昨天做了什么。

模板：

> Yesterday I ______. I also ______.

示例：

> Yesterday I saw a bird. I also ate a sandwich.

一周计划：

星期重点动词示例
周一see / eatI saw a dog. I ate rice.
周二go / playI went to the park. I played with my friend.
周三have / doI had a test. I did my homework.
周四make / takeMom made dinner. It took 30 minutes.
周五run / swimI ran in the garden. I swam in the pool.
周六复习写3个句子，用不同的过去式
周日自由写写一段话（3-5句），描述周末做了什么

五、过去式儿歌/顺口溜（帮助记忆）

> See-saw, see-saw（跷跷板）

> I see today, I saw before.

>

> Go-went, go-went

> I go now, I went before.

>

> Eat-ate, eat-ate

> I eat now, I ate before.

>

> Do-did, do-did

> I do now, I did before.

可以每天读一遍，边读边拍手打节奏。

like doing 类动词学案

Verbs + doing (Gerund)

一、概念说明

在英语中，有些动词后面要接 动词-ing形式（动名词） 作宾语。常见的有：

动词意思例句
like喜欢I like swimming.
love热爱She loves singing.
enjoy享受/喜欢We enjoy playing games.
hate讨厌He hates getting up early.
finish完成They finish doing homework at 5.
go去做（活动）I go shopping on Sunday.
start / begin开始It starts raining.
stop停止Please stop talking.

注意：

  • like / love / hate 也可以接 to do (动词不定式)，含义基本相同：

- I like swimming. = I like to swim.

  • 但有些动词只能接 doing：

- enjoy doing ✅ enjoy to do ❌

- finish doing ✅ finish to do ❌

否定句： 在动词前加 don't / doesn't

一般疑问句： 用 Do / Does 提问

二、翻译练习——将下列句子翻译成英文

  1. 我喜欢游泳。

→ I like swimming.

  1. 她热爱唱歌。

→ She loves singing.

  1. 我们喜欢玩游戏。

→ We enjoy playing games.

  1. 他讨厌早起。

→ He hates getting up early.

  1. 他们五点完成作业。

→ They finish doing homework at 5.

  1. 我星期天去购物。

→ I go shopping on Sunday.

  1. 请停止说话。

→ Please stop talking.

四、选词填空——用所给动词的正确形式填空（注意加 -ing）

  1. I like ___________ (swim) in summer.
  2. She enjoys ___________ (read) books.
  3. He hates ___________ (get) up early.
  4. They finish ___________ (do) their homework at 6 o'clock.
  5. We go ___________ (shop) every weekend.
  6. Please stop ___________ (run) in the classroom.
  7. My father loves ___________ (watch) football on TV.

五、选择填空

  1. I like ___________ basketball. （A. play B. playing C. to playing）
  2. She enjoys ___________ to music. （A. listening B. listen C. listens）
  3. He hates ___________ vegetables. （A. eating B. eat C. eats）
  4. We finish ___________ class at 4:30. （A. have B. having C. has）
  5. They go ___________ every Saturday. （A. swim B. swimming C. swims）
  6. Please stop ___________ loudly. （A. talk B. talking C. talks）
